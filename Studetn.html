  1. 2 Series Gran Coupe:

    2. The BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe (F74) is a compact four-door sedan with a sporty design and a focus on driving dynamics.
    It features a 1.5-liter 3-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine producing 154 bhp and 230 Nm of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.
    The car also includes features like a digital key, boost mode for rapid acceleration, and a suite of driver-assistance systems.

    Engine and Performance:
    Engine: 1.5-liter 3-cylinder TwinPower Turbo petrol
    Displacement: 1499 cc
    Max Power: 154 bhp @ 4900-6000 rpm
    Max Torque: 230 Nm @ 1500-4600 rpm
    Transmission: 7-speed Steptronic dual-clutch automatic
    0-100 km/h: 8.6 seconds
    ARAI Mileage: 16.35 kmpl
    Fuel Type: Petrol

  2. 3 Series (including Gran Limousine and M340i)

    3. The BMW 3 Series is a compact executive car with a range of engine options , varying power outputs, and available in both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. Key specifications include a choice of 4-cylinder or 6-cylinder engines, with power ranging from 187 to 382 bhp, and torque from 400 to 500 Nm. The 3 Series also boasts features like an 8-speed automatic transmission, limited slip differential (in some models), and various driver assistance systems.

    Engine and Performance: Engine Options: The 3 Series offers both 4-cylinder and 6-cylinder engines.
    Engine Type: Examples include the B48 Turbocharged I4.
    Power: Power output can range from 187.74 to 382.19 bhp.
    Torque: Torque figures range from 400 Nm to 500 Nm.
    Transmission: Typically an 8-speed automatic transmission with manual override and paddle shifters, according to YouTube.
    Drive Type: Available in rear-wheel drive (RWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD).
    Acceleration: Some models can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in around 6.2 seconds.
    Fuel Type: Petrol.
    Other Notable Specifications:
    Fuel Tank Capacity: 59 liters.
    Seating Capacity: Typically 5 seats in 2 rows.
    Doors: 4 doors.
    Suspension: Double-joint spring-strut axle in front and a five-link axle in the rear.
    Tyres: Run-flat tires and alloy wheels are common.
    Dimensions: Length: 4823 mm, Width: 2068 mm, Height: 1441 mm.
    Wheelbase: 2961 mm.
    Turning Radius: 6 meters.
    Features:
    Sunroof: Electrically adjustable sunroof.
    Driver Assistance: Parking assist, cruise control, parking sensors (front and rear), reverse camera with guidance.
    Seating: Electrically adjustable driver's seat with memory presets.
    Other: Heated front seats, rain-sensing wipers, speed-sensing door lock.

  3. 5 Series

    4. The BMW 5 Series is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its sporty performance and refined interior.
    Key specifications include engine options ranging from 2.0L to 3.0L, with power outputs from 187 to 375 bhp and torque from 350 to 620 Nm.
    It also features an 8-speed automatic transmission and offers both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive options.

    Engine and Performance:
    Engine Options: The 5 Series offers a variety of engine choices, including 2.0L 4-cylinder and 3.0L 6-cylinder engines, both petrol and diesel.
    Power and Torque: Power output ranges from 187.74 to 375 bhp, with torque figures between 350 Nm and 620 Nm, according to CarDekho and Car and Driver.
    Transmission: An 8-speed automatic transmission is standard, with options for rear-wheel drive (RWD) and all-wheel drive (xDrive).
    Acceleration: 0-60 mph acceleration times vary by model, with the 530i at 5.8 seconds and the 540i xDrive at 4.4 seconds, according to BMW USA.
    Dimensions and Features: Dimensions: The BMW 5 Series has a wheelbase of 117.9 inches and a length of 199.2 inches, according to Car and Driver.
    Interior: The interior features a curved display with a 14.9-inch touchscreen and a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster.
    Other Features: The car includes ambient lighting, a wireless charging pad, and four-zone climate control.
    Optional Features: Options include a sunroof, a heated steering wheel, and sport seats.
